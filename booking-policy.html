Booking Policy

Last updated: 29 October 2025
This Booking Policy outlines how to book tours with Agadir Local Guide, payment procedures, cancellation terms, and what to expect during the booking process. For detailed terms and conditions, please see our Terms & Conditions.

1) How to Book

You can book a tour or excursion with us through the following methods:

  • Online: Use our contact form to request a booking. Include your preferred tour, dates, number of participants, and any special requirements.
  • Phone: Call us at +212 601-820422 to speak directly with our team.
  • WhatsApp: Message us via WhatsApp for quick booking inquiries.
  • Email: Contact us via email (available through our contact page).
Tip: For same-day or next-day bookings, we recommend calling or using WhatsApp for the fastest confirmation.

2) Required Information

When booking, please provide:

  • Full names of all participants
  • Ages of all participants (for age-appropriate pricing and safety requirements)
  • Preferred tour date and time
  • Number of participants
  • Hotel or accommodation name and address (for pickup if included)
  • Contact information (phone, email, WhatsApp)
  • Any special requirements, dietary restrictions, or medical conditions we should know about
  • Pickup location (if different from your hotel)

3) Booking Confirmation

  • Once we receive your booking request, we will confirm availability and send you a booking confirmation via email, WhatsApp, or phone (as preferred).
  • Your booking is confirmed when we acknowledge it and, where applicable, payment has been received or arranged.
  • Please review your confirmation carefully and contact us immediately if any details are incorrect.
  • If your requested date/time is not available, we will suggest alternative options.

4) Payment Options

Payment Methods:

  • Cash: Cash payment (Euros or Moroccan Dirhams) on the day of the tour, either at pickup or at the tour location.
  • Bank Transfer: Advance payment via bank transfer (details provided upon booking).
  • Online Payment: Some tours may accept online payment methods (check at booking).
  • Deposit + Balance: For some tours, a deposit may be required to secure your booking, with the balance due on the day of the tour.

Payment Schedule:

  • Payment terms will be confirmed at the time of booking.
  • For advance bookings, full payment may be required 48 hours before the tour date.
  • For last-minute bookings, payment is typically due on the day of the tour.
Currency: Prices may be quoted in EUR, MAD (Moroccan Dirham), or USD. Confirm the currency at booking. Exchange rates may apply.

5) Pricing & What's Included

Pricing:

  • Prices are generally per person unless stated otherwise (e.g., "private tour" prices may be per group).
  • Children's prices apply as specified on each tour page. Age ranges for child pricing vary by tour.
  • Group discounts may be available for larger groups—please inquire.

Typically Included:

  • Professional local guide
  • Hotel pickup and drop-off (as specified for each tour)
  • Transportation (as specified)
  • Safety equipment (helmets, life jackets, etc., where applicable)
  • Activities/experiences as described

Typically NOT Included (unless stated):

  • Meals and drinks (unless specified as "lunch included")
  • Entrance fees to attractions or national parks (check tour description)
  • Tips for guides and drivers (gratuities are appreciated but optional)
  • Personal expenses
  • Travel insurance
  • Items not explicitly listed as included

6) Free Cancellation Policy

Most tours offer free cancellation up to 24 hours before the scheduled start time. Check the specific tour page for details.
  • More than 24 hours before: Full refund or reschedule (subject to availability).
  • Less than 24 hours before or no-show: No refund unless due to weather or operational cancellation by us.
  • Refunds will be processed within 14 business days using the original payment method.
  • For force majeure events (e.g., severe weather, natural disasters), we will offer rescheduling or a full refund.

7) Changes to Bookings

  • You may request changes to your booking (date, time, number of participants) subject to availability.
  • Changes more than 48 hours before: Usually accommodated at no extra charge.
  • Changes less than 48 hours before: May incur fees or may not be possible due to operational constraints.
  • Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to make changes.

8) Pickup & Meeting Points

  • Hotel Pickup: For tours that include pickup, we collect you from your hotel or accommodation in Agadir. Please provide your hotel name and address when booking.
  • Pickup Time: Pickup times will be confirmed in your booking confirmation. Please be ready at the designated time.
  • Meeting Point: Some tours may meet at a central location instead of hotel pickup. Details will be provided.
  • Punctuality: We aim to be punctual. If we are delayed, we will contact you. Please ensure your contact number is correct.
  • If you miss pickup due to your delay, we cannot guarantee you can join the tour (no refund applies).

9) Group Sizes & Availability

  • Minimum Participants: Some tours require a minimum number of participants to operate. If the minimum is not met, we may cancel and offer a full refund or alternative options.
  • Maximum Participants: Group sizes may be limited for safety and quality. Private tours can often accommodate custom group sizes.
  • Private Tours: Available on request. Private tours offer flexibility in timing, itinerary, and group size (subject to capacity).
  • Availability is confirmed on a first-come, first-served basis.

10) Age Requirements & Children

  • Age requirements vary by tour (check individual tour pages).
  • Children must be accompanied by a parent or guardian unless otherwise specified.
  • Some activities have minimum age restrictions (e.g., quad biking may require participants to be 16+).
  • Infants (under 2) may be free or at a reduced rate—please inquire.
  • Child safety seats or equipment will be provided where applicable.

11) Special Requirements

Please inform us at the time of booking if you have:

  • Medical conditions or mobility requirements
  • Dietary restrictions or allergies (for tours including meals)
  • Pregnancy (some activities may not be suitable)
  • Disabilities (we will do our best to accommodate, but some tours may have limitations)
  • Language preferences (we offer tours in multiple languages—please specify)

We will advise if we can accommodate your requirements or suggest suitable alternatives.

12) Weather & Safety

  • If weather conditions make a tour unsafe, we reserve the right to cancel and will offer a reschedule or full refund.
  • For outdoor activities, light rain may not cancel a tour unless safety is compromised.
  • You will be notified of cancellations due to weather as soon as possible.
  • Your safety is our priority. We follow safety protocols and provide necessary equipment.

13) What to Bring

Recommended items (check your specific tour for details):

  • Comfortable clothing and footwear appropriate for the activity
  • Sun protection (hat, sunglasses, sunscreen)
  • Water bottle
  • Camera or phone for photos
  • Valid ID (passport or copy)
  • Cash for personal expenses, tips, or optional extras
  • Any medications you need

14) Contact During Your Tour

If you need to contact us during your tour:

  • Call +212 601-820422
  • Message us via WhatsApp at +212 601-820422
  • Speak directly with your guide (they will have our contact information)

15) Complaints & Feedback

We strive to provide excellent service. If you have concerns during your tour:

  • Speak with your guide immediately—many issues can be resolved on the spot.
  • After your tour, contact us via the contact page or call us within 48 hours.
  • We take all feedback seriously and will investigate and respond promptly.
  • Positive reviews are also appreciated and help other travelers!

16) Questions?

If you have questions about booking, payment, or our tours, please don't hesitate to contact us:

  • Phone: +212 601-820422
  • WhatsApp: +212 601-820422
  • Email/Contact Form: Contact Page
