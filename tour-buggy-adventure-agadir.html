Complete Itinerary: Buggy Adventure Agadir

Step 1: Pickup & Base Arrival (30 minutes)

Hotel Pickup & Safety Briefing

We collect you from your accommodation in our comfortable transport vehicle. Arrive at our buggy adventure base where you'll meet your guide, receive safety equipment (helmet, goggles, bandana), and get a comprehensive safety briefing. Learn the controls, practice techniques, and ask any questions. First-timers spend extra time getting comfortable with their buggy before departure.

Step 2: Desert Dunes Exploration (45-60 minutes)

Ride Through Golden Sand Dunes

Start your adventure by conquering the stunning sand dunes! Feel the power of your buggy as you climb up and down golden slopes. Stop at the highest point for panoramic desert views and epic photo opportunities. Your guide will show you the best riding lines and photo spots. This is where the real fun begins!

Step 3: Wild Beach Riding (30-45 minutes)

Atlantic Coast Quad Adventure

Experience the unique thrill of buggy adventure along pristine wild beaches with Atlantic waves crashing beside you! Feel the sea breeze as you ride along the coast, splash through shallow water pools (seasonal), and enjoy the contrast of ocean and desert. This section is often guests' favorite part of the tour!

Step 4: Berber Village & Tea Break (20-30 minutes)

Cultural Stop & Refreshments

Take a break at a traditional Berber village. Meet local families, enjoy authentic Moroccan mint tea, and learn about Berber culture and daily life in the desert. Rest, rehydrate, and soak in the peaceful village atmosphere. This cultural touch makes our buggy tours unique!

Step 5: Optional Sandboarding (20-30 minutes - Combo Tour Only)

Sandboarding Adventure

For combo tour guests: Try sandboarding down massive dunes! Our guide will teach you the technique, provide boards, and ensure your safety. It's easier than it looks and incredibly fun. Great workout and amazing photos guaranteed!

Step 6: Return Journey (30 minutes)

Drop-off at Your Hotel

Complete your buggy adventure and return to the base. Quick rinse-off available to clean sand and dust. We'll drive you back to your hotel in Agadir or Taghazout, arriving with incredible memories, amazing photos, and the biggest smile!

Essential Guide: Buggy Adventure in Agadir

üìã What to Wear & Bring:
  • Clothing: Long pants recommended (protects from sun and sand). Comfortable t-shirt or long sleeves. Avoid loose clothing that could get caught.
  • Footwear: Closed-toe shoes essential (sneakers perfect). NO sandals or flip-flops allowed for safety.
  • Sun protection: Sunscreen (SPF 50+), sunglasses with strap, bandana or buff for dust protection
  • Camera: Bring your phone/camera in a secure bag. GoPros welcome! Guide will help with photos.
  • Extras: Small backpack, water bottle, light jacket (mornings/evenings can be cool)
‚ÑπÔ∏è Important Information:
  • Age requirement: Drivers must be 16+ years old. Children 6-15 can ride as passengers with an adult driver (‚Ç¨25)
  • License: No driving license required for buggys in Morocco

  • Physical fitness: Moderate fitness required. You'll be holding on and maneuvering the quad
  • Pregnancy: Not recommended for pregnant women due to bumpy terrain
  • Health conditions: Not suitable for those with back/neck problems or recent injuries
  • Weather: Tours run rain or shine. We provide rain gear if needed
  • Free cancellation: Up to 24 hours before departure for full refund
