Experience the Magic of the Desert

Escape from the hustle and bustle of Agadir in favor of the tranquil Agafay Desert. This unforgettable overnight experience combines adventure, culture, and relaxation in one of Morocco's most stunning landscapes.

On the day of the activity, we'll pick you up from your chosen meeting point in Agadir to begin our adventure to the desert of Agafay. After a quick 1-hour scenic drive, we'll venture into the Atlas Mountains' expansive, arid landscapes and make our way to our luxury desert camp.

Upon arrival, you'll receive a warm welcome with a traditional cup of Moroccan mint tea as you settle into your beautifully appointed haima. Depending on the time you choose to start the tour, you can either relax in the camp to watch the spectacular sunset over the dunes or embark on an optional camel ride through the desert's golden sands.

As evening falls and the desert transforms under the starlit sky, we'll gather for a delicious traditional Moroccan feast. The dinner includes authentic Moroccan salads, succulent chicken tajine, aromatic vegetable couscous, and traditional desserts. While you dine, you'll be entertained by a fantastic traditional show featuring mesmerizing belly dancing, thrilling fire-eaters, and soulful Gnawa music!

After an evening of exceptional food, spectacular entertainment, and wonderful desert views, you'll retire to your private haima to rest underneath the clear, starry skies of the tranquil desert. The experience of sleeping under millions of stars in the desert silence is truly magical and unforgettable.

In the morning, wake up to witness the breathtaking sunrise painting the desert in golden hues. After enjoying a lovely wholesome breakfast featuring fresh bread, jams, Moroccan pancakes, and fresh orange juice, we'll make our way back to Agadir, arriving in the late morning.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1 - Afternoon (4:00 PM - 5:00 PM)

Pickup & Journey to Desert

We'll pick you up from your hotel in Agadir and begin our scenic 1-hour journey to the Agafay Desert. Enjoy beautiful views of the Atlas Mountains and changing landscapes as we leave the city behind.

Day 1 - Late Afternoon (5:00 PM - 6:30 PM)

Arrival at Desert Camp

Arrive at our luxury desert camp and receive a warm welcome with traditional Moroccan mint tea. Check into your private haima, relax by the pool (seasonal), and watch the stunning desert sunset. Optional camel ride available.

Day 1 - Evening (7:30 PM - 10:00 PM)

Traditional Dinner & Entertainment

Enjoy a sumptuous Moroccan feast under the stars featuring authentic salads, chicken tajine, vegetable couscous, and traditional desserts. Be entertained by a spectacular show with belly dancing, fire-eaters, and live Gnawa music.

Day 1 - Night (10:00 PM onwards)

Sleep Under the Stars

Retire to your comfortable private haima for a peaceful night's sleep under the crystal-clear desert sky. Each haima is equipped with comfortable beds, private bathroom, and all necessary amenities.

Day 2 - Morning (7:00 AM - 9:00 AM)

Sunrise & Breakfast

Wake up early to witness the magical desert sunrise. Enjoy a traditional Moroccan breakfast with fresh bread, homemade jams, Moroccan pancakes, and fresh orange juice.

Day 2 - Late Morning (9:00 AM - 10:30 AM)

Return to Agadir

After breakfast, we'll begin our journey back to Agadir, arriving at your hotel in the late morning with unforgettable memories of your desert experience.
