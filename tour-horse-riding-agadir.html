Arrive at our welcoming ranch facility where you'll meet your professional riding instructor. You'll be introduced to your horse - carefully selected to match your riding ability and temperament. Scenic Beach & Countryside Horse Ride

After this wonderful horse riding Agadir experience, your driver will return you comfortably to your hotel in Agadir or Taghazout. Total experience time: approximately 2-2.5 hours from pickup to drop-off.
